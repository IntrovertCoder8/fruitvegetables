FRUITS

What is a fruit?

In a botanical sense, a fruit is the fleshy or dry ripened ovary of a flowering plant, enclosing the seed or seeds.

  • Apple
  • Banana
  • Grape
  • Orange
  • Watermelon

VEGETABLES

What is vegetables?

Vegetables are parts of plants that are consumed by humans or other animals as food.

  • Carrot
  • Corn
  • Cucumber
  • Onion
  • Potato
